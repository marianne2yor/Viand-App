About

Viand is your ultimate companion in discovering new recipes, designed to satisfy your cravings and help you find the perfect viand. Whether you're in the mood for a classic Filipino dish or something new, Viand allows you to search for recipes that cater to your dietary needs and preferences. With access to the Edamam API, you can explore a variety of dishes, get detailed nutritional information, and discover trending recipes. Whether you're craving Adobo or looking for a fresh twist on Sinigang, Viand makes it easy to find the perfect recipe to elevate your meal planning!
